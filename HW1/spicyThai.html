Thai Peanut Sauce

Ingredients

  • sesame oil
  • as many cloves of garlic as you want
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sirarcha
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • ground ginger

Process

Begin by heating up your preferred amount of sesame oil in a small pot. Once it's warm add the garlic and cook for a couple of minutes. When you get tired of that you can start adding the liquid ingredients at your leisure. I usually start with the peanut butter and then the honey because it acts as a nice base for the rest of the ingredients. The last thing you want to add is the ginger because you don't need a lot and honestly, if you forget it, this should still be good. I just do a few dashes and stir it in. Mix the sauce with your main ingredients immediately as this can look pretty unappetizing when left to sit.
