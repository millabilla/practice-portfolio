Cheese Sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded cheese
  • 1.5 cups milk or maybe a smaller amount of heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons of flour OR 1 tablespoon of corn starch
  • 2 tablespoons of butter

Process

Grab a smaller pot and warm the butter over medium heat. Once it's melted add your flour or cornstarch and then start whisking like your life depends on it. You should be looking at what looks like inedible clumps of half melted glue. Good. Now would probably be a good time to season that. And then quick before you burn it, begin adding the milk S-L-O-W-L-Y in small increments and whisk as you go. You want the mixture to thicken back up each time you add milk and whisk. Keep going until you're out of milk but honestly this part always feels like it takes forever. It's normal. Once you have a nice thick sauce, you're goint to start adding the shredded cheese, clump by clump. Wait for it to melt and mix in completely before adding more. Once all the cheese is melted use it immediately or feel free to let it harden and eat it like pudding. Judgement free zone here.
