Bone Apple Tea

Recipes for people that have a vague idea of how to make food

Welcome to my recipe page Bone Apple Tea, a website for people who have a vague notion of how to cook but maybe just aren't sure where to start. I have carefully curated three sauces since I began going to school many moons ago. All you have to do is follow my instructions to the best of your ability and you're cooking with *fire. It is important to note that I do not actually measure any of my ingredients and I'm just going off of muscle memory.

I have used these sauces on everything from pasta to zoodles to meat and pan fried vegetables. In some cases, like the marinara sauce, I have just eaten it by itself like a spicy chunky tomato soup. Click on "Getting Started" to see what main items you will absolutely need as well as some main staples I always have on hand for each sauce. Then feel free to wander on over to the "Sauces" section to begin cooking.

*The author of this page would like to point out that unless you're literally cooking with fire, there should be no fire. Please be aware of your surroundings.
